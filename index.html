PROJECT SAMPLE
This is the fourth year engineering project conducted in the University of Manchester. The team consists of Ekram, Daniel, Hani, Sanket, Shihab and Hussein. The aim of the team is to create a multi-purpose semi autonomous vehicle which will for the moment be used to detect land mines and to assess the health of grass. Experimental pictures of the buggy has been attached above.
The main mode of control for the buggy will be through the internet through a browser web-page. This decision was mainly to make it so that the control was platform independant since almost all smart devices nowadays such as phones and tablets come equipped with a web-browser.
